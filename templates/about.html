About Us

Our Story

The CGS IBDP Gazette is a student-led digital magazine, a vibrant hub of journalism and creativity within the Costeas-Geitonas School's IB community. Born from a vision to amplify student voices, we strive to capture the essence of IB life, both within our school walls and in the broader global context.

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to showcasing the remarkable projects, insightful research, and diverse achievements of CGS students. Our platform offers monthly insights into school events, discussions on pertinent global issues, and a spotlight on the unique experiences that shape our learners. We believe in the power of student journalism to connect, inform, and inspire.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to empower CGS IB students to express themselves freely, exchange diverse perspectives, and celebrate the journey of learning through compelling journalism, unbridled creativity, and collaborative spirit. The CGS IBDP Gazette is committed to fostering open-mindedness, cultivating curiosity, and strengthening our community bonds. We aim to be a true reflection of the IB values that guide us all as thoughtful, globally aware, and principled individuals.

Contribute Your Voice

The Gazette thrives on the passion and insights of our student body. Do you have an exciting experience to share, an event that deserves attention, or a research topic you'd like us to explore? Perhaps you've penned an article yourself and are looking for a platform to publish it.

Our submission form is your direct channel to the Gazette. We welcome all forms of contributions – from quick tips and ideas to fully developed articles. Your participation is vital in shaping the narrative of our IB community. Share your unique voice and make a lasting impact!
